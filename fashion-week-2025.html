Fashion Week 2025

Become a top stylist and create stunning looks for the most glamorous event of the year!

Game Details

  • Genre: Fashion, Dress Up, Simulation
  • Controls: Mouse or Touch controls
  • Platform: Browser (PC & Mobile)

About Fashion Week 2025

Fashion Week 2025 is a creative fashion styling game where you take on the role of a top stylist preparing for the most prestigious event in the fashion world. Style five stunning models in high-fashion looks across five unique themes, from elegant Chanel-inspired outfits to bold punk rock ensembles.

The game features:

  • Five unique fashion themes to explore
  • Hundreds of couture dresses, accessories, and hairstyles to mix and match
  • Realistic model customization options
  • Stunning visual presentation with runway showcases
  • Progression system to unlock new fashion items

Unleash your creativity, perfect your styling skills, and take over the fashion world. Are you ready to rule the runway?

How to Play

Use your mouse (on PC) or tap (on mobile) to select models, browse through clothing options, and create your stunning fashion combinations. Start by choosing a model, then select from various categories including dresses, tops, bottoms, accessories, and hairstyles to create the perfect look.

Each theme has specific fashion requirements, so make sure to read the brief before styling your models. Once you're satisfied with your creation, send your model to the runway and watch as they showcase your design to the fashion world!

The more creative and theme-appropriate your designs, the better your reception will be from the virtual fashion critics.
