Cubes 2048 IO

Merge cubes with the same numbers to create higher values and dominate the arena!

Game Details

  • Genre: IO, Puzzle, Multiplayer
  • Controls: Mouse or Touch controls
  • Platform: Browser (PC & Mobile)

About Cubes 2048 IO

Cubes 2048 IO is an exciting multiplayer game inspired by the classic 2048 puzzle, but with a competitive twist. In this game, you control a cube with a number on it and move around the arena, collecting smaller cubes and merging with cubes that have the same number as yours.

The game features:

  • Real-time multiplayer gameplay against players from around the world
  • Simple but addictive merging mechanics
  • Leaderboard system to track the top players
  • Colorful graphics and smooth controls
  • Strategy-based gameplay that's easy to learn but difficult to master

Can you create the largest cube and top the leaderboard? Play now and find out!

How to Play

Use your mouse to control your cube's movement (on mobile, simply touch and drag). Move around to collect smaller cubes to grow, and find cubes with the same number as yours to merge and double your value.

Be strategic about which cubes you merge with - larger numbers mean more points, but also make you a bigger target for other players. Avoid larger cubes than yours, as they can absorb you and end your game.

The goal is to become the biggest cube on the map by strategically merging with other cubes and avoiding threats.
