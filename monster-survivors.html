Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this action-packed roguelike survival game!

Game Details

  • Genre: Action, Roguelike, Survival
  • Controls: WASD to move, Mouse to aim and shoot
  • Platform: Browser (PC & Mobile)

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense roguelike survival game where you must battle against endless waves of increasingly difficult monsters. As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience and unlock powerful upgrades that will help you survive longer.

The game features:

  • Procedurally generated levels ensuring each playthrough is unique
  • Dozens of different enemy types with unique attack patterns
  • Various character abilities and weapons to unlock
  • RPG-style progression system with permanent upgrades
  • Challenging boss fights that will test your skills

How long can you survive against the monster horde? Play now and find out!

How to Play

Use the WASD keys to move your character around the map. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies. Collect experience orbs dropped by defeated monsters to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones.

Survival is key - try to avoid getting surrounded and use your special abilities wisely. The longer you survive, the stronger you'll become, but the monsters will also grow in number and power.
